Why CivicNet?

Most proof-of-work cryptocurrencies have quietly become a race between massive ASIC farms. CivicNet follows a different philosophy by making mining accessible to ordinary CPU hardware through the custom civiclight algorithm.

Current civiclight is CPU-friendly rather than fully ASIC-resistant. A future upgrade toward a memory-hard version is already planned as part of the project's roadmap.
